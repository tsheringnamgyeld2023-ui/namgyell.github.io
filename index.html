Learning Framework

The Learning Framework serves as the foundational structure that shapes all activities and educational processes at The Royal Academy. It works in harmony with the Bhutan Baccalaureate Learning Process, creating a dynamic and reciprocal relationship.

Five Areas of Development

At our school, education goes beyond textbooks and exams. We focus on Cerebral, Emotional, Physical, Social, and Spiritual development.

Cerebral Development

Strengthening thinking, learning, and analytical skills through English, Dzongkha, Mathematics, Sciences, ICT, and more.

Emotional Development

Helping students understand and manage their feelings positively while building confidence and emotional awareness.

Physical Development

Promoting fitness, hygiene, and teamwork through sports and physical activities.

Social Development

Encouraging positive relationships, social responsibility, and moral values.

Spiritual Development

Inspiring mindfulness, compassion, and inner peace guided by Buddhist values.
